**Jeff T Byrd** is a web developer, composer and sound artist based in Athens. Trained on piano and saxophone, his works employ free improvisation, extensive sampling and collage techniques. He performs and releases music under his own name and with [Budokan Boys](https://budokanboys.club/). He is also a member of [SPROING](https://www.instagram.com/sproingsounds/), a free-improvised music collective in Vienna.
